Online and In Person
The in-person and online courses at METSE help with taking your courses where ever you are by providing you with the flexibility to take your courses at a time and place that is convenient for you. Whether you are taking your courses at our campus, online, or at a location near you, we offer the same quality education and support to help you succeed.
Mentoring
At METSE, our personalized course will improve and boost kids' thinking by providing them with an individualized education that is tailored to their specific needs and abilities. This will allow them to reach their full potential and become independent, critical thinkers.
Blockchain Support
The NFT's that we sell on the blockchain will help to spread the word about METSE to a wider audience, not just in the United States, but all around the world. The profits generated from the sale of these NFT's will help us to cover the costs of the items necessary for tutoring, such as books, materials, and other resources. This will allow us to reach even more students and help them to succeed in their studies.
